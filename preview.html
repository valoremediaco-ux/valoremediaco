about valoré media

founded by
vale orduy.

Valoré Media is a Miami-based creative agency specializing in elevated content creation, social media strategy, and creative direction for fashion, beauty, lifestyle, and elevated brands.

With a marketing background, fashion business studies in Milan, and international business studies in Vienna, Vale brings a global, editorial perspective to every project. Her experience covering international fashion weeks — from New York to Milan to Miami — shapes a refined approach where strategy and storytelling work together, so content feels effortless, aspirational, and aligned with each brand's identity.

From campaign shoots and styling to social media management and brand storytelling, every detail is curated to create a cohesive and elevated online presence designed to connect, grow, and leave a lasting impression.
